Brief Bio

I am currently a Junior Research Fellow at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur.

My research focuses on making AI sustainable and developing sustainable AI systems. I work under the supervision of Prof. Niloy Ganguly and Prof. Saptarshi Ghosh. My work aims to develop methods and frameworks to measure, analyze, and reduce the energy footprint of AI models across their lifecycle - spanning training, fine-tuning, and inference.

Currently, I am working on the problem of Chain-of-Thought compression as well as understanding the intricacies of LLM Reasoning in non-English text.
